Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the meet & greet?
We'll spend 30-45 minutes getting to know you and your dog, discussing their routine, preferences, and any special needs. This helps me create a personalized care plan and ensures your dog is comfortable with me before we start regular walks.
How do you handle emergencies?
I'm pet first aid certified and always carry emergency supplies. I have your vet's contact information and emergency contacts readily available. In case of any incident, you'll be contacted immediately along with appropriate medical professionals if needed.
Can I track my dog's walk?
Yes! You'll receive real-time GPS tracking during walks, plus photos and detailed notes about your dog's behavior, bathroom breaks, and any notable interactions. Updates are sent via text message after each walk.
