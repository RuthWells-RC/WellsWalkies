My Story

Growing up, I always had dogs in my family. From my childhood Golden Retriever, Buddy, to the rescue Beagle mix I adopted in college, dogs have been my constant companions and greatest teachers.

After working in corporate environments for several years, I realized my true calling was working with animals. I started volunteering at local shelters, which opened my eyes to the incredible need for quality pet care services in our community.

Wells Walkies was born from a simple belief: every dog deserves individual attention, exercise, and love, especially when their humans can't be there. I've built this business on trust, reliability, and genuine care for each four-legged client.

When I'm not walking dogs, you can find me hiking local trails, reading about animal behavior, or volunteering at weekend adoption events. I currently share my home with two rescue dogs, Luna and Charlie, who keep me on my toes and remind me daily why I love this work so much.
